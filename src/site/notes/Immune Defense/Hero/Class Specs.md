## Field Passive
##### Infiltrator

You can now perform the `Hide` action by interacting with three types of environmental objects, which are now called 'Hiding Spots'. Doing so will hide yourself inside it, making you invisible with limited vision and actions for the duration. There is an audible and visual cue when entering, exiting, as well as randomly while inside. 

Each object additionally provide a unique utility:
- Boxes: You may move at a very reduced speed.
- Vents: You may teleport and switch to other nearby vents. 
- Lockers: Your vision is not as limited, with less frequent audible and visual cues.
